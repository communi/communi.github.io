bool IrcNumericMessage::composed
read
Since
3.3

This property holds whether the message is composed.

  • RPL_MOTDSTART, RPL_MOTD, and RPL_ENDOFMOTD are composed as IrcMotdMessage
  • RPL_NAMREPLY and RPL_ENDOFNAMES are composed as IrcNamesMessage
  • RPL_TOPIC and RPL_NOTOPIC are composed as IrcTopicMessage
  • RPL_INVITING and RPL_INVITED are composed as IrcInviteMessage
  • RPL_WHOREPLY is composed as IrcWhoReplyMessage
  • RPL_CHANNELMODEIS is composed as IrcModeMessage
  • RPL_AWAY, RPL_UNAWAY, RPL_NOWAWAY are composed as as IrcAwayMessage
